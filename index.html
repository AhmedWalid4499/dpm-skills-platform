DPM Skills
Profile Platform

The internal tool for Delivery Project Managers to document their skills and for managers to understand team capability — structured, visual, and always up to date.

Why this platform exists

Skills visibility across
the Delivery Practice

Within Orange Business, DPMs bring a wide range of expertise — from Agile delivery to enterprise tooling to stakeholder management. This platform creates a single, structured way to capture and communicate that expertise internally, so managers always have an accurate picture of their team's capability.

  • 🎯

    Skills-first internal profiles

    Go beyond job titles. Capture certifications, tools, and competency depth in one structured profile that managers can actually use.

  • 📊

    Instant competency visibility

    Radar charts and readiness scores make it easy for managers to assess strengths and growth areas without lengthy reviews.

  • 🏢

    Team-level intelligence for managers

    Aggregate your team's profiles into a single dashboard to identify skill gaps, plan training, and make informed staffing decisions.

  • ⚡

    Simple and self-serve

    DPMs complete their own profile in minutes. No IT setup, no external systems, no login required — everything runs in the browser.
